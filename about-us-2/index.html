About Us

We Tailor-made your Logistics needs!
Serving our Clients and Partners Since 2005
Trust us with your business! We are going the extra-miles for you, so that you can concentrate with your core-business

Who We Are

Since our humble beginning in 2005, our founder/CEO is having a clear vision to create a seamless logistics solution. We understand that logistics can be challenging for most people. Being a trustworthy agent/partner with integrity is the cornerstone and paramount in this industry.

We believe that technology is our friend and a solution for the future. We invest on people and cutting-edge technology to help our clients and partners to enjoy the direct benefit.

Our teams of professionals are passionate with logistics. We offer a full-range logistics solutions: Sea/Air Freight Forwarders, NVOCC, custom brokerage, land transportation, e-commerce solution, marine insurance and bonded and distribution center warehousing to name a few.0

Our fully-owned offices and sub-agents covers nationwide: Jakarta, Semarang, Surabaya, Belawan/Medan, Bali, Makassar, Batam and other parts of Indonesia.

As an accredited member of Indonesia Logistics and Forwarders Association (ILFA), WCA Family, the Universal Freight Organisations (UFO), ALFA Family Network and holding U.S. FMC Foreign NVOCC Ocean Transport Intermediary (OTI) License (FMC Organization# 025282), we are able to deliver an end-to-end and seamless worldwide logistics solutions.

Your business is also safe and secure with our Freight Services Liabilities (FSL) insurance.

What We Provide

A complete logistics solution for your needs to and from Indonesia.

To our Clients: we are a professional forwarder with a strong integrity without any heavy price-tag.
To our Overseas Partners: we are a professional and trustworthy partner with fast communication response,
mutual business and financial integrity.
